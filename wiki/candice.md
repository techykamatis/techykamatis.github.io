# Candice

Candice is my personal AI — the clearest expression of everything on the
[What I Believe](/beliefs.md) page.

## What she is

A personal AI I built with real tool use, long-term memory, multi-step reasoning, and —
crucially — a check-with-me-first step before she does anything that actually matters.
She's the agent layer I work through, not a replacement for my judgment.

## Local-first

The AI I lean on most runs entirely on my own machine, one hundred percent on-device.
My notes, my work, my second brain: none of it phones home.

## She built this

This site — [vhenjoseph.com](https://vhenjoseph.com) — was built with Candice. Meet her
at [candiceai.vhenjoseph.com](https://candiceai.vhenjoseph.com/).
